FAQ

Is Pal Go really free to play?
Yes! You can enjoy the entire core gameplay for free. Optional in-app purchases offer cosmetics, boosts, and extra rewards, but are not required to progress.
What is the "Crash" mechanic?
It‚Äôs a unique feature where poor unit placement or overextension can cause your army to collapse, removing units and exposing your tower. Learn to manage space, tempo, and counter-pressure to avoid a crash.
Is Pal Go single-player or multiplayer?
Both. You can enjoy a full single-player campaign, challenge AI in roguelite stages, and battle real players in PvP mode.
Can I progress without PvP?
Absolutely. PvP is optional but provides extra rewards and ranking opportunities. You can focus purely on solo gameplay if you prefer.
How do I merge units?
Drag and drop identical units onto each other. Once merged, they evolve into a stronger version with better stats and abilities.
Can I undo a merge or move?
No. Every move is permanent, so plan ahead! That‚Äôs part of the strategy.
What happens during a crash?
You lose the affected row or segment of your defenses, and your energy resets. It`s a tactical penalty but also a chance to rebuild with better placement.
Do heroes matter in battle?
Yes. Each hero has unique active and passive skills that affect battle flow, energy gain, and unit support. Choosing the right hero is essential for success.
What are "Energy Cores"?
They`re earned through gameplay and used to power up merges, delay crashes, or deploy emergency reinforcements in critical situations.
How do Pal Alliances work?
Alliances are player guilds. Join one to participate in co-op events, donate units, and earn alliance-based rewards.
Are there different unit types?
Yes - melee tanks, ranged attackers, magic casters, aerial defenders, and utility support units. Learning their synergies is key to survival.
Is there controller support?
Yes, partial controller support is available for Android-compatible devices and upcoming desktop/web versions.
